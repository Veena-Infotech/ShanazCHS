Security

we ensure top-tier security with 24/7 gated security and CCTV surveillance, offering residents a safe and protected environment. The building has modern fire safety systems to handle emergencies efficiently. Additionally, controlled access entry ensures only authorized individuals can enter the premises. With a dedicated security team monitoring the surroundings, residents can enjoy peace of mind.

Comfort & Convenience

Shanaz Apartment is designed to provide residents with the utmost comfort and convenience. The spacious 3 BHK apartments offer ample living space, complemented by high-speed elevators for smooth accessibility across floors. The property ensures uninterrupted power backup and a reliable water supply from both municipal and borewell sources.

Recreational & Wellness Amenities

Shanaz Apartment offers an excellent selection of recreational and wellness amenities designed for a comfortable and enriching lifestyle. Residents can unwind in beautifully landscaped gardens, providing a serene environment for relaxation. The children’s play area ensures a safe and engaging space for kids to enjoy outdoor activities.

Sustainability & Smart Living

Shanaz Apartment is designed with a strong focus on sustainability and smart living, integrating eco-friendly solutions to enhance urban living. The property features rainwater harvesting systems, helping conserve water and reduce dependency on external sources. Energy-efficient lighting is installed throughout the premises to minimize electricity consumption, contributing to a greener environment.
